Little Nightmares follows a small girl in a raincoat who wakes up wth no memory in a strange and dark place. Only armed with an unnatural and insatible hunger, "Six", must traverse through the immense underwater resort known as "The Maw". While escaping dangerous resort staff encounters, whom are trying to capture and eat her, and solving puzzles, Six slowly begins uncovering the horrendous truth about the resort and why her hunger is so unquenchable.
